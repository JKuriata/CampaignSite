Trinity Campus

An architectural marvel in the centre of Dublin, Trinity campus served as the headquarters of the Scientists for decades. Filled with books on every topic on the planet, an array of scientific tools and weapons, and an unknown amount of artifacts, this campus is a hub for Scientific knowledge. Abandoned by the majority of the Scientists during the Wall incident, the campus is now home to only a few Scientific Individuals.
The campus itself is protected by a series of scientific wards and rituals, designed to keep the knowledge and artifacts inside safe and unused.
